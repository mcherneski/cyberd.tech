Before the deeper protocol work, I spent a stretch building the small, useful things web3 communities actually run on: automation and bots for EthSafari, a community NFT proof of concept for MetaCamp, and a handful of integrations around them.

Most of it was for community or equity rather than a paycheck, honestly more than it should have been. But it's how I got fluent in the ecosystem, and it's a fair snapshot of how I like to work: find the friction, ship something that removes it, move on.
