Moxa helps people with variable income — freelancers, contractors, independent earners — budget against paychecks that don't look the same twice.

I co-founded it with my wife and own the technical direction. The platform runs on AWS and is defined entirely in Terraform, which keeps the infrastructure reviewable and reproducible. Underneath the product is a few years of research into behavioral finance, user needs, and where applied AI actually helps versus where it just adds noise.

The engineering goal has stayed constant: give a small team the ability to move quickly without accumulating the kind of debt that quietly caps a company later.
