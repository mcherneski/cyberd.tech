Alternun is one of the more interesting problems I've touched: tokenizing gold reserves that stay in the ground, rather than digging them up first. The whole model only works if the on-chain representation maps honestly onto a physical, verifiable reserve — which makes the design questions unusually strict.

I worked with them in an advisory and engineering capacity — taken in equity — reasoning through how a real-world asset like this should be represented, backed, and enforced on-chain.
